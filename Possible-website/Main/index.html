Summary for Hillsborough College Resources

Hillsborough College offers a variety of resources to support students in their academic and personal growth. These resources include:

  • Academic Advising: Students can meet with academic advisors to discuss their course selections, degree requirements, and career goals.
  • Academic Resources: The college provides tutoring services, writing centers, and other academic support to help students succeed in their coursework.
  • Financial Aid: The college provides information and assistance with financial aid applications and scholarship opportunities.
  • Library Resources: The library offers a wide range of books, journals, and online databases for research and study purposes.
  • Counseling Services: Students can access counseling services for mental health support, stress management, and personal development.
  • Career Resource Center: The career center provides resources for job searching, resume writing, and interview preparation.
