Academic Advising

Academic advising at Hillsborough College is designed to help students navigate their educational journey and achieve their academic goals.
Here are some benefits of academic advising:

  • Personalized academic planning and course selection guidance
  • Assistance with degree requirements and academic progress tracking
  • Support for career exploration and goal setting
  • Resources for academic success and study skills development
  • Referrals to campus resources and support services
  • Help with scheduling classes for the next term

If you'd like to contact to schedule an appointment, you can reach out to the academic advising office at (813) 253-7000 or visit their website by clicking Here.
