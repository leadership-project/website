Academic Resources

Hillsborough College offers a variety of academic resources to support students in their educational journey. These resources include:

  • Tutoring Services: The college provides tutoring services in various subjects to help students improve their understanding and performance in their courses.
  • Writing Center: The writing center offers assistance with writing assignments, including brainstorming, organizing ideas, and improving grammar and style.
  • Math Lab: The math lab provides support for students taking math courses, offering help with homework, test preparation, and understanding mathematical concepts.
  • Study Groups: Students can join or form study groups to collaborate with peers and enhance their learning experience.
  • Online Resources: The college offers access to online resources such as e-books, academic journals, and research databases to support students' academic work.

This is also the same place they offer academic advising. and counseling services.
