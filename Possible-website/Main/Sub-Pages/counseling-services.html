Counseling Services

Hillsborough College offers counseling services to support students' mental health and well-being. These services include:

  • Individual Counseling: Students can meet with a counselor for one-on-one sessions to discuss personal issues, stress management, and mental health concerns.
  • Group Counseling: The college offers group counseling sessions on various topics such as stress reduction, anxiety management, and building healthy relationships.
  • Crisis Intervention: In case of emergencies or immediate mental health concerns, students can access crisis intervention services for support and guidance.
  • Workshops and Programs: The counseling center hosts workshops and programs focused on topics like mindfulness, self-care, and coping strategies.
  • Referrals: If necessary, the counseling center can provide referrals to external mental health professionals or resources for additional support.

For more information about counseling services or to schedule an appointment, you can contact the counseling center at brcounseling@hccfl.edu for brandon campus, or visit their website by clicking Here.
Also make sure to check out the Student Assistance Program.

If you or someone you know might be in a crisis or immediate danger, please contact helpline at:

  • Sucide Prevention Lifeline: 988
  • Substance Abuse Helpline: 1-800-662-HELP
  • 911 If In Immediate Danger
