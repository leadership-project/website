Financial Aid

Financial aid is available to help students cover the costs of their education at Hillsborough College. The financial aid office provides information and assistance with various types of financial aid, including:

  • Grants: Grants are a form of financial aid that does not need to be repaid. They are typically awarded based on financial need.
  • Scholarships: Scholarships are awarded based on various criteria, such as academic achievement, extracurricular involvement, or specific talents. They also do not need to be repaid.
  • Loans: Loans are a form of financial aid that must be repaid with interest. They can help cover educational expenses but should be used responsibly.
  • Work-Study Programs: These programs provide students with part-time employment opportunities on campus to help earn money for their education.

The financial aid office can assist students with the application process, provide information about eligibility requirements, and offer guidance on how to maximize their financial aid opportunities.
