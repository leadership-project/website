Library Resources

Hillsborough College's library offers a wide range of resources to support students in their academic and research endeavors. These resources include:

  • Books and Journals: The library has an extensive collection of books, academic journals, and magazines covering various subjects and disciplines.
  • Online Databases: Students can access a variety of online databases that provide access to scholarly articles, research papers, and other academic resources.
  • Study Spaces: The library provides quiet study areas, group study rooms, and computer workstations for students to use for their academic work.
  • Research Assistance: Librarians are available to assist students with research questions, help them navigate the library's resources, and provide guidance on citation styles.
  • Interlibrary Loan Services: If the library does not have a specific resource, students can request it through interlibrary loan services, allowing them to access materials from other libraries.
