Career Resource Center

Each campus of Hillsborough College has a Career Resource Center that provides students with resources and support for their career development.
The career centers offer a variety of services, including:

  • Job Search Assistance: The career centers provide resources and guidance for students who are looking for internships, part-time jobs, or full-time employment opportunities.
  • Resume Writing Support: Students can receive assistance with creating and improving their resumes to enhance their chances of securing job interviews.
  • Interview Preparation: The career centers offer workshops and one-on-one coaching to help students prepare for job interviews and improve their interview skills.
  • Career Counseling: Students can meet with career counselors to discuss their career goals, explore different career paths, and receive guidance on how to achieve their professional aspirations.
  • Career Exploration: The career centers provide resources and tools for students to explore different career options and make informed decisions about their future.
  • Workshops and Events: The career centers host various workshops and networking events to help students develop their professional skills and connect with potential employers.
  • Clothes To Borrow: The career centers may offer clothing items for students to wear during job interviews or other professional settings.

You can find the Career Resource Center in Brandon Campus in 210 in the Student Services Building. For more information or to schedule an appointment, you can contact the career center at (813) 253-7830 or visit their website by clicking Here.
